Biryani Recipe

Dum Chicken Biryani

A beautiful marriage of chicken, rice, spices, and aromatics. Fused to create a sensational mixture of flavours. One of the most prized dishes of the South Asian Peninsula

Ingredients

  • 1 Whole Chicken
  • 4 White Onions
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 TBSP Salt
  • 1 Whole Lemon
  • 2 cups of White Basmati Rice
  • Shan Biryani (bombay, hydrabdi, etc) Spice Mixture
  • Handful of Coriander
  • Olive Oil
  • 2 TBSP Garlic Paste
  • 2 TBSP Ginger Paste

Steps

  1. Cut all onions and place in a large pot. Add Olive Oil until Onions are fully covered. Place on Medium heat. Cook until golden brown
  2. Add cut tomatoes, chicken, garlic and ginger paste, and Shan Biryani Spice Mixture. Mix until coated well. Cover and cook until chicken is 3/4 cooked.
  3. While chicken is cooking. Cook rice till half cooked.
  4. Once Chicken is cooked, squeeze half a lemon in to the pot. Mix and let sit
  5. Pre-heat oven to 275
  6. In a foil pan, layer rice and chicken mixture. Cover with foil wrap
  7. Place foil pan in oven, let cook for 15-20 mins
  8. Remove pan, mix the inner contents. Add coriander on top
  9. EAT IT ALL
