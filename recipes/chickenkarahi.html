Chicken Karahi

South Asian delicacy of whole chicken slow cooked in mixture of spices blended into beautiful tomato marinade.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Oil
  • 1 Whole Chicken
  • 3 Tomatoes
  • 5 Green Chillies
  • 1/2 tbsp Chilli Powder
  • 1 tbsp Coriander Powder
  • 1/2 tbsp Cumin Powder

Steps

  1. In a wok, add oil and salt
  2. Deep fry chicken in oil for 3-4 minutes
  3. Add in ginger and garlic paste, cook it till rawness is gone
  4. Add tomatoes and water, give it a mix, cover, and cook for 4-5 minutes
  5. Once tomatoes are soft, remove tomato skin, and mash the tomatoes with a spatula
  6. Add green chilies and the spices like red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  7. Add curd and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  8. Add black pepper powder, dried fenugreek leaves, salt, and mix well.
  9. Cook for a further 5 minutes and the chicken karahi is ready.
